## Packages
framer-motion | Complex animations and page transitions
recharts | Visualizing poll results and quiz data
date-fns | Formatting dates for articles and chat
clsx | Utility for conditional classes

## Notes
Chat uses Server-Sent Events (SSE). The hook `useChatStream` handles the stream reading.
Auth is handled via Replit Auth (useAuth hook).
German political theme colors (Black, Red, Gold) used as accents.
